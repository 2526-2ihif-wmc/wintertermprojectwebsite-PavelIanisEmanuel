Honor


Honor is a Chinese smartphone brand that was originally a sub-brand of Huawei but became independent in 2020. The company focuses on delivering innovative technology at competitive prices, particularly targeting young consumers and tech enthusiasts.

Honor phones are known for their excellent value for money, featuring premium designs, powerful processors, and advanced camera systems. The brand has gained significant market share in Europe and Asia with its Magic series flagships and budget-friendly X and N series devices.

Unlike its former parent company Huawei, Honor has full access to Google Mobile Services, making it a compelling alternative for users who want Chinese innovation with Western app compatibility. Honor's MagicOS provides a feature-rich Android experience with AI capabilities.
