OnePlus


OnePlus is a premium smartphone brand under the BBK Electronics umbrella, known for delivering flagship specifications at competitive prices. The brand's motto "Never Settle" reflects its commitment to providing high-performance devices with clean software and premium build quality.

OnePlus phones feature OxygenOS (based on Android), fast charging technology, and flagship processors. The brand has partnered with Hasselblad for camera tuning and offers full Google Mobile Services support in global markets. OnePlus is particularly popular among tech enthusiasts and power users.
