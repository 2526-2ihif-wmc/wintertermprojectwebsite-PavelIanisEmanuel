Apple



Apple is a consumer tech company based in California, USA.
Their phones are called "iPhones" and are really popular among Americans. They have their own mobile operating system called iOS. There's a lot of critique that the phones are extremely overpriced and that they don't offer new features. On the AI side, Apple is several years behind with Apple Intelligence turning out to be a huge fail. A major downside of all iPhones are the slow charging speeds at a maximum of 40W on the most expensive phones.
Buy these phones if you only care about the phone brand and not about the features. If you have the money and really want an Apple device, buy a phone of the 17 series. Don't buy the 16e or the Air which in 2025 only offer one camera. The naming scheme is a number that increments each year, though no iPhone 2, 3 or 9 exists.
