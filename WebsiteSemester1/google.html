Google



Google is a consumer tech company based in California, USA. First entering the market with great value for money, now they focus on high-end phones. They're known for the minimalism and good feature packs. Their phones are called "Pixel". The software, stock Android, features tons of AI features. Google was the first company to introduce such functionalities to their phones.
Click for a complete list of all Pixel devices ever released

10 Pro Fold

Google's foldable and most expensive phone. First foldable ever to feature an IP68 certification. Supports Qi2 15W wireless charging. 48MP main camera and a 5015mAh battery. The screen size when unfolded is 8". Buy if you want Google's nice software inside a foldable.

10 Pro, 10 Pro XL

The XL version is, like the name suggests, just a bigger version of the normal Pro. These are Google's high-end phones. The Pro charges like the Fold, whereas the XL charges with 45W wired and 25W wireless charging.

10

Apple's attempt at a thin phone. Turned out to be a massive flop due to its singular camera for 1000€ and a tiny battery (3,149 mAh) that barely lasts half of what a normal phone lasts. Hard to recommend phone due to its compromised functionality.

9a

Apple's attempt at a budget phone, though 600€ isn't truly budget. At that high price, you only get 1 camera and a huge notch which takes up a lot of space on the front screen. A better choice would be the Google Pixel 10 or the Samsung Galaxy S25 FE.
