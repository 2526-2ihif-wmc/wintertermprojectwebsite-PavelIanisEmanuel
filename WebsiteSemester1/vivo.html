Vivo



Vivo is a subsidiary of BBK focused on phones and other accessories.

Vivo is known for the good value for money in cheaper phones and for the crazy specs in more expensive ones, especially in regard to cameras.
Click for a complete list of all Vivo phones ever released or down below to access each series description with one click.





X series


The X series is Vivo's most high-end series. The primary focus is the cameras. The generation count used to increment by 10 every year (80, 90, 100) but after 100 it increases by 100 every year (100, 200, 300,...).

V series


The V series is Vivo's mid-range phone series. There is no fixed naming scheme like Samsung's A series, but a higher number generally means a better phone. The slightly cheaper Vivo phones are known for their mesmerizing colors and design on the back, especially making it a good choice for younger people. Not all V series phones are included for the sake of simplicity.
