Nothing



Nothing is a London-based consumer technology company founded by Carl Pei in 2020. The company focuses on creating unique, transparent designs with distinctive Glyph Interface lighting systems. Nothing phones run on Nothing OS, a clean Android-based operating system.

Nothing is known for its innovative design philosophy, featuring transparent backs that showcase internal components. Their phones are celebrated for excellent build quality, competitive pricing, and a near-stock Android experience with useful customizations. The brand has two lines: Nothing for premium devices and CMF by Nothing for budget-friendly options.






Nothing


The Nothing series represents the company's flagship line, featuring the iconic Glyph Interface - a unique LED lighting system on the back that serves as notifications, ringtones, and visual effects. These phones offer premium build quality with transparent designs that showcase the internal components.

CMF by Nothing


CMF by Nothing is the budget sub-brand that brings Nothing's design philosophy to a more affordable price range. While these phones don't feature the Glyph Interface, they maintain excellent build quality and run the same clean Nothing OS. CMF phones are known for their modular design elements and vibrant color options.
