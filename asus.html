Allview


Allview is a Romanian electronics brand founded in 2002 and based in BraÈ™ov, Romania. The company is owned by Visual Fan and is known for being one of the first Romanian companies to develop smartphones and tablets under its own brand. Allview's motto "Live. Dream. Experience." reflects its mission to make technology accessible to everyone.

Allview phones run on Android with their custom Visual UI interface and were particularly popular in Romania and Eastern Europe. The brand was second in Romanian smartphone market share in 2015, behind only Samsung. Notable innovations include the X2 Soul (once the world's slimmest smartphone at 5.5mm) and the X4 Soul Infinity with AVI, the first voice assistant supporting the Romanian language. While the brand has reduced smartphone production in recent years to focus on premium devices and smart home solutions, their legacy devices remain beloved for their value and Romanian engineering.

Soul (X Series) - Flagship Innovation


The Soul series (also known as X series) represents Allview's premium flagship lineup, featuring cutting-edge technology and Romanian innovation. These phones were designed to compete with international flagship devices while maintaining competitive pricing. The series included groundbreaking devices like the X2 Soul (world's slimmest phone in 2014) and the X4 Soul Infinity with AVI voice assistant in Romanian.

P Series - Mid-range Value


The P series was Allview's main mid-range lineup offering excellent value for money. These phones provided solid performance, good cameras, and reliable battery life at accessible prices. The P series was extremely popular in Romania and Eastern Europe for everyday users who wanted a dependable smartphone without flagship prices.

V Series - Premium Alternative


The V series (Viper) was Allview's premium alternative lineup, featuring high-quality displays (often AMOLED), premium materials like Gorilla Glass, and solid performance. The V1 Viper was a turning point for Allview, being one of the best-selling products and establishing the brand's reputation for quality Romanian engineering.

A Series - Entry Budget


The A series represents Allview's entry-level and budget lineup, designed to bring smartphones to everyone at ultra-affordable prices. These phones offer basic functionality, decent displays, and good battery life for users who need a simple, reliable device for calls, messaging, and light usage. Perfect as first smartphones or backup devices.
