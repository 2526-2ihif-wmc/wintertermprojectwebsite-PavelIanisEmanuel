Realme


Realme is a fast-growing Chinese smartphone brand under the BBK Electronics umbrella, known for delivering exceptional value for money. The brand targets young, budget-conscious consumers with feature-packed devices at aggressive prices. Realme's motto "Dare to Leap" reflects its ambitious approach to technology and innovation.

Realme phones feature Realme UI (based on Android), powerful processors from Qualcomm and MediaTek, massive batteries, and fast charging technology. The brand has partnerships with RICOH for camera tuning and offers full Google Mobile Services support globally. Realme is particularly popular in Asia for its unbeatable price-to-performance ratio.
