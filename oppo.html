Oppo


Oppo is a leading Chinese smartphone manufacturer known for its camera innovation and sleek designs. As the parent company of OnePlus and partner with Hasselblad, Oppo has established itself as a premium brand focusing on photography, fast charging technology, and sophisticated hardware.

Oppo phones feature ColorOS (based on Android), impressive camera systems often co-engineered with Hasselblad, and industry-leading fast charging technology. The brand is particularly popular in Asia and Europe, offering full Google Mobile Services support in global markets.
