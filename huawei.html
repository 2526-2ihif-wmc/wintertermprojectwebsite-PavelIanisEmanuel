Huawei


Huawei is a Chinese technology giant known for its innovative smartphones, telecommunications equipment, and consumer electronics. Despite US sanctions limiting access to Google Mobile Services, Huawei has developed its own HarmonyOS ecosystem and continues to lead in camera technology and design innovation.

Huawei phones run on HarmonyOS (their proprietary operating system) and feature cutting-edge camera systems developed in partnership with Leica. The company is particularly strong in the Chinese market and offers unique foldable designs and premium flagship devices. Note: Huawei phones sold outside China do not have Google Play Services.
