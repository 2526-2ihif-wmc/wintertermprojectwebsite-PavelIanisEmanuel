Google



Google is a consumer tech company based in California, USA. First entering the market with great value for money, now they focus on high-end phones. They're known for the minimalism and good feature packs. Their phones are called "Pixel". The software, stock Android, features tons of AI features. Google was the first company to introduce such functionalities to their phones.


Click for a complete list of all Pixel devices ever released

10 Pro Fold

Google's foldable and most expensive phone. First foldable ever to feature an IP68 certification. Supports Qi2 15W wireless charging. 48MP main camera and a 5015mAh battery. The screen size when unfolded is 8". Buy if you want Google's nice software inside a foldable.

10 Pro, 10 Pro XL

The XL version is, like the name suggests, just a bigger version of the normal Pro. These are Google's high-end phones. The Pro charges like the Fold, whereas the XL charges with 45W wired and 25W wireless charging. They have 50MP main cameras.

10

Base model featuring the temperature sensor and 50MP main camera like the Pro models. Charges up like the Pro and has a 4970mAh battery. It's rather compact with a size of 6.3". Like all Pixels of this gen, it's powered by Google's own Tensor G5 chip.

9a

10a will be released in a few months. This is Google's budget phone at just 250-500€. A special feature is the all flat back with no camera bump. The display size is 6.3" and the battery has a capacity of 5100mAh, which is bigger than the 10 Pro and 10 Pro Fold.
