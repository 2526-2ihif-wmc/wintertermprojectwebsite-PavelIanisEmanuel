iQOO


iQOO is a sub-brand of Vivo focused on performance and gaming smartphones. The brand name stands for "I Quest On and On" and targets young, tech-savvy consumers who demand flagship performance at competitive prices. iQOO has quickly gained recognition for delivering powerful specifications without the premium price tag.

iQOO phones are known for their exceptional gaming capabilities, featuring advanced cooling systems, high refresh rate displays, and powerful processors. The brand offers full Google Mobile Services support and competes directly with other gaming-focused brands like Realme GT and OnePlus.
