Xiaomi



Xiaomi is a huge China-based company focused on consumer tech. They produce not only devices like phones, tablets, watches, TVs and buds but also smart home appliances and EVs.

Xiaomi users report high reliability and durability of their phones. The company is known for its very good price to performance ratio and the great value for money. There are 3 phones brands: Xiaomi, Redmi and Poco. Poco actually went independent a few years ago, but they're still tightly associated with Xiaomi and share software skins. There also used to be a 4th phone brand. Black Shark was a gaming focused brand that went independent of Xiaomi. The last time they released a phone was 2022.
Xiaomi also provides AI features in the gallery app and of course carry all the Android AI features.
